01 / About Me

Building the future,
one circuit at a time.

I'm Gyamerah Afriyie Philimon Junior, a high school science student from Ghana obsessed with robotics, drones, and embedded systems. Building machines that physically interact with the world is what gets me out of bed every morning.

My passion goes beyond just building cool things — I see robots as real solutions to real-world challenges. From life-saving drones to smart IoT safety devices, every project I work on is designed with purpose.

I'm working toward a Mechatronics Engineering degree — the perfect intersection of mechanics, electronics, and computer science. I actively participate in robotics clubs, honing teamwork and problem-solving skills through hands-on builds and competition.

Looking to connect with professionals, mentors, and collaborators who share a vision for an automated, robotics-driven future.
